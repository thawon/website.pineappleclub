Services

Family day care

We offer education and care services provided by a registered educator with diploma qualification and 3 years of experience in a long day care centre. Our educator is supported by Integricare Family Day Care St George. The environment is setup for early childhood learning to create an atmosphere that is safe, welcoming and engaging. We help children develop skills, interests and become independent and confident learners. We are approved to administer Child Care Benefit (CCB) and Child Care Rebate (CCR).

Opening: Mon - Fri 7:30AM - 6:00PM
Group age: 10 months - 5 years old

Before/after school care

We provide long and flexible opening hours between 7:30AM to 6:00PM Monday to Friday. With a great location just behind Bexley Public School, we ideally situated to provide before/after school care service in the area. To find out more, come and speak to our experience educator.

Opening before school: Mon - Fri 7:30AM - 9:00AM
Opening after school: Mon - Fri 3:30PM - 6:00PM
Group age: School age

Vocation care

At Pineapple Club, we accommodate parents who require child care on a non-casual, ad hoc basis during the day or after working hours. Saturday and Sunday care can also be arranged. We have a strong and fun educational activity program that your child will enjoy. Contact us to discuss your needs, we are confident that our experienced educator will be able to assist you.

Opening: Mon - Fri 7:30AM - 6:00PM
Sat - Sun can be arranged
Group age: 10 months - school age
