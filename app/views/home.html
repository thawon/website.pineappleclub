Welcome

Pineapple Club is a Family Day Care (FDC) registered with Integricare Family Day Care St George. Our FDC is operated by a trained educator who has years of experience in child care with diploma qualification. We are approved to administer Child Care Benefits (CCB) and Child Care Rebate (CCR)

Our FDC key features

  • 90% of the space is setup for childcare service
  • Educator is supported by high quality FDC organisation Integricare with every need
  • Your child will receive maximum attention due to our low child-educator ratio. They will better develop and bond with their educator
  • Our diploma qualified educator has over 3 years of experience providing care in a well-established long day care centre
  • Strong educational programs
  • Activity report is provided to parent on daily basis
